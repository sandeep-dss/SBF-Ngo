About Our Visionary Director :

Our Director - Santa Kumari Bapatla "lady With A Good Heart" Was Born In A Remote Village. Santa Kumari Bapatla Is A Well-known Social Worker. At An Early Age, Santa Was Inspired By Her Father And Observed The Suffering Of People, And Decided To Dedicate Herself To Compassionately Serving Humanity From A Place Of Inner Peace And Strength. This Is Evident In Her Name; She Learned To Give Of Herself At An Early Age So That She Could Receive Herself Again At A Later Stage In Life. Santa's First Major Contribution Was Serving People, Her Approach To Serving Others Today Resembles How She Took Care Of Many Kids From Other Orphanages. She Began Her Blood Donation Work Early In Life. Her Work Continues Today To Help The Less Fortunate. Santa's Ongoing Work Is Inspiring Others To Help Those In Need By Donating Money Or Time Toward Service Projects. The Fact That Santa Bapatla Foundation's Work Has Ongoing Effects Makes It Even More Impactful Than If It Had Started Working On Its Projects Today And Is Willing To Continue Indefinitely.

Our Vision :

"Alone we can do so little; together we can do so much."
- Helen Keller

Welcome To The Santa Bapatla Foundation, A Non-profit Organization That Is Committed To Serving The Community And Making A Difference In The Lives Of People. We Are Dedicated To Supporting Various Causes And Initiatives That Address Critical Social Issues And Improve The Well-being Of Individuals And Families.
1. We Run An Orphanage That Provides Care, Support, And Shelter For Children And Elders Who Are In Need. We Strongly Believe In The Importance Of Family And Community, And Our Goal Is To Create A Safe And Nurturing Environment That Fosters Love, Compassion, And Understanding.
2. Our Foundation Also Encourages Women's Empowerment And Believes That Women Should Have Equal Opportunities To Achieve Their Goals And Dreams. We Provide Resources And Support To Help Women Develop Their Skills, Knowledge, And Confidence To Succeed Personally And Professionally.
3. We Recognize That The Youth Are The Future Of Our Society, And We Are Committed To Providing Them With The Tools And Resources They Need To Succeed. Our Skill Development Programs Help Young People Develop Valuable Skills And Knowledge That Are Essential In Today's Rapidly Changing World.
4. Our Foundation Also Supports Farmers, Who Are The Backbone Of Our Society. We Provide Them With The Necessary Resources, Tools, And Training To Help Them Overcome The Challenges They Face And Achieve Success.
5. Finally, We Help Those In Need During Natural Calamities By Providing Essential Supplies, Shelter, And Other Resources. We Believe In Standing With The Community During Difficult Times And Working Together To Build A Better Future.
We Invite You To Join Us In Our Mission To Serve The Community And Create A Better World For Everyone. Together, We Can Make A Difference And Build A Brighter Future For All.
Our Mission: 100 Destitute Children Shelter & Education By 2024.
